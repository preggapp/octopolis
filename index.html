What is Octopolis

She's two things at once.

Octopolis is a 25-30 foot tall illuminated octopus with interactive arms stretching 40-50 feet — a large-scale metal sculpture with integrated lighting, sensors, and multi-sensory elements inside and outside the form.

Her head is a communal gathering space: ocean sounds, bubble effects, cooling misters, and an overhead view of her three glowing hearts. Her eyes are built from locally sourced driftwood.

After Burning Man, she goes into the ocean. Disassembled and redeployed as a platform for coral reef restoration — a home for marine life displaced by habitat loss. Art that doesn't end when the burn is over.
